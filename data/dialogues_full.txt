--- At the restaurant ---
A: 你好吗？ (nǐ hǎo ma) — How are you?
B: 我很好。 (wǒ hěn hǎo) — I am fine.
A: 你要吃什么？ (nǐ yào chī shén me) — What do you want to eat?
B: 我很喜欢吃米饭。我不喜欢吃鱼。 (wǒ hěn xǐ huān chī mǐ fàn wǒ bù xǐ huān chī yú) — I really like eating rice. I don't like eating fish.
A: 我们去饭店吃饭吧。 (wǒ men qù fàn diàn chī fàn ba) — Let's go eat at a restaurant.
B: 好，我们一起去吧。 (hǎo wǒ men yì qǐ qù ba) — OK, let's go together.

--- Talking about the weather ---
A: 今天天气怎么样？ (jīn tiān tiān qì zěn me yàng) — How is the weather today?
B: 今天很热。 (jīn tiān hěn rè) — It is very hot today.
A: 明天会下雨吗？ (míng tiān huì xià yǔ ma) — Will it rain tomorrow?
B: 我觉得今天很冷。 (wǒ jué de jīn tiān hěn lěng) — I think it is very cold today.
A: 我们去看电影吧。 (wǒ men qù kàn diàn yǐng ba) — Let's go watch a movie.
B: 好，我们马上出发吧。 (hǎo wǒ men mǎ shàng chū fā ba) — OK, let's set off right away.

--- At the hospital ---
A: 你要去医院吗？ (nǐ yào qù yī yuàn ma) — Do you need to go to the hospital?
B: 是的，他必须去医院。 (shì de tā bì xū qù yī yuàn) — Yes, he must go to the hospital.
A: 他在医院里吗？ (tā zài yī yuàn lǐ ma) — Is he in the hospital?
B: 他在哪里？ (tā zài nǎ lǐ) — Where is he?
A: 他在医院里。 (tā zài yī yuàn lǐ) — He is in the hospital.
B: 我们马上出发吧。 (wǒ men mǎ shàng chū fā ba) — Let's set off right away.

--- Making plans ---
A: 你明天准备做什么？ (nǐ míng tiān zhǔn bèi zuò shén me) — What are you planning to do tomorrow?
B: 我明天要考试。 (wǒ míng tiān yào kǎo shì) — I have an exam tomorrow.
A: 你在学习汉语吗？ (nǐ zài xué xí hàn yǔ ma) — Are you studying Chinese?
B: 是的，我在学习汉语。 (shì de wǒ zài xué xí hàn yǔ) — Yes, I am studying Chinese.
A: 我们一起学习吧。 (wǒ men yì qǐ xué xí ba) — Let's study together.
B: 好，你的朋友在哪里？ (hǎo nǐ de péng yǒu zài nǎ lǐ) — OK, where is your friend?

--- Getting to know someone ---
A: 你认识那个人吗？ (nǐ rèn shí nà ge rén ma) — Do you know that person?
B: 我不认识他。你认识她吗？ (wǒ bù rèn shí tā nǐ rèn shí tā ma) — I don't know him. Do you know her?
A: 他是我朋友。 (tā shì wǒ péng yǒu) — He is my friend.
B: 你的爱好是什么？ (nǐ de ài hào shì shén me) — What are your hobbies?
A: 我喜欢唱歌跳舞。你喜欢运动吗？ (wǒ xǐ huān chàng gē tiào wǔ nǐ xǐ huān yùn dòng ma) — I like singing and dancing. Do you like sports?
B: 是的，我想认识你的朋友。 (shì de wǒ xiǎng rèn shí nǐ de péng yǒu) — Yes, I would like to meet your friend.
