--- At the restaurant ---
A: 你好吗？ — How are you?
B: 我很好。 — I am fine.
A: 你要吃什么？ — What do you want to eat?
B: 我很喜欢吃米饭。我不喜欢吃鱼。 — I really like eating rice. I don't like eating fish.
A: 我们去饭店吃饭吧。 — Let's go eat at a restaurant.
B: 好，我们一起去吧。 — OK, let's go together.

--- Talking about the weather ---
A: 今天天气怎么样？ — How is the weather today?
B: 今天很热。 — It is very hot today.
A: 明天会下雨吗？ — Will it rain tomorrow?
B: 我觉得今天很冷。 — I think it is very cold today.
A: 我们去看电影吧。 — Let's go watch a movie.
B: 好，我们马上出发吧。 — OK, let's set off right away.

--- At the hospital ---
A: 你要去医院吗？ — Do you need to go to the hospital?
B: 是的，他必须去医院。 — Yes, he must go to the hospital.
A: 他在医院里吗？ — Is he in the hospital?
B: 他在哪里？ — Where is he?
A: 他在医院里。 — He is in the hospital.
B: 我们马上出发吧。 — Let's set off right away.

--- Making plans ---
A: 你明天准备做什么？ — What are you planning to do tomorrow?
B: 我明天要考试。 — I have an exam tomorrow.
A: 你在学习汉语吗？ — Are you studying Chinese?
B: 是的，我在学习汉语。 — Yes, I am studying Chinese.
A: 我们一起学习吧。 — Let's study together.
B: 好，你的朋友在哪里？ — OK, where is your friend?

--- Getting to know someone ---
A: 你认识那个人吗？ — Do you know that person?

A: 他是我朋友。 — He is my friend.
B: 你的爱好是什么？ — What are your hobbies?

B: 是的，我想认识你的朋友。 — Yes, I would like to meet your friend.
